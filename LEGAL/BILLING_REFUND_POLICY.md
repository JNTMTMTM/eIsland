# Billing & Refund Policy / 计费与退款政策

Last Updated: 2026-04-23

## EN
1. Billing Cycle
- Plans are billed monthly/yearly in advance unless otherwise stated.

2. Renewal
- Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled before renewal date.

3. Refund
- Refund window: 14 days from first payment for eligible plans.
- No partial refunds for consumed enterprise/support services unless agreed.

4. Failed Payments
- Access may be suspended after grace period 10 days.

5. Taxes & Invoices
- Taxes are charged where required.
- Invoice requests: billing@mail.shicthrs.com

6. Chargeback
- Fraudulent or abusive chargeback may result in suspension.

7. Suspension/Ban and Billing
- If an account is suspended or banned due to terms violation, access to paid features may be restricted during the action period.
- Unless otherwise required by law or explicitly agreed, suspension/ban caused by user violation does not by itself constitute a refund reason.
- Billing disputes related to enforcement actions can be submitted to billing@mail.shicthrs.com for case-by-case review.

## 中文
1. 计费周期
- 除非另有说明，套餐按月/年预付费。

2. 续费
- 订阅默认自动续费，需在续费日前取消。

3. 退款
- 符合条件的套餐可在首次付款后 14 天内申请退款。
- 已消耗的企业服务/支持服务一般不支持部分退款，除非另有约定。

4. 扣费失败
- 宽限期 10 天后可能暂停服务访问。

5. 税费与发票
- 按适用法规计税。
- 发票申请联系：billing@mail.shicthrs.com

6. 拒付
- 欺诈或滥用拒付行为可能导致服务暂停。

7. 暂停/封禁与计费
- 如账号因违反条款被暂停或封禁，在处置期间其付费功能访问可能受限。
- 除非法律另有要求或双方另有明确约定，因用户违规导致的暂停/封禁本身不当然构成退款理由。
- 与处置措施相关的计费争议可发送至 billing@mail.shicthrs.com，我们将按个案复核。
